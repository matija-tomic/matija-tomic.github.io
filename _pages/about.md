I'm Matija, a first-year PhD student under supervision of Prof. [Elina Robeva](https://personal.math.ubc.ca/~erobeva/) at the [Department of Mathematics](https://www.math.ubc.ca/), University of British Columbia, Canada. I am affiliated with the [Institute of Applied Mathematics](https://www.iam.ubc.ca/). My research sits at the intersection of multilinear algebra, algebraic geometry, and signal processing.

Before moving to Vancouver, I obtained my Master's degree under supervision of Prof. [Lieven de Lathauwer](https://homes.esat.kuleuven.be/~delathau/) at the [Department of Electrical Engineering](https://www.esat.kuleuven.be/english), KU Leuven, Belgium. My thesis titled 'Sparse Approximation and Sets of Polynomial Equations' explored sparse approximation through the lens of structured polynomial systems and tensor decompositions.
